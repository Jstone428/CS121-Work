IMPACT: Personal Safety

Sophomore year of high school, my mom had me enrolled in a self-defense class. This self-defense class was not of a martial arts style. IMPACT was a women's self-defense class, meaning that it was specifically tailored to women and focused on our strengths. I participated in this class for 6 weeks. After taking this class, I am more confident in myself. IMPACT holds the fundamental truth that “your life is worth fighting for”. IMPACT is empowering, and I encourage women, and even men, to take their classes. This class is what sparked my desire to study WGSS.
